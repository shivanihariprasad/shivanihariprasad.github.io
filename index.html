As a PhD student at University of California, San Diego advised by Dr. Kimberly Claffy, my research focuses on internet security and performance, with a strong interest in the potential and applications of Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites. My work aims to enhance our understanding of global connectivity and develop innovative approaches to studying satellite communication networks. I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers at CAIDA . Previously, I worked at Trellix(formerly McAfee) as a Software Development Engineer for three years, where I worked on a product called ePolicy Orchestrator. I graduated with a Bachehlor's of Engineering in Computer Science from RV College of Engineering, Bangalore, India in 2019.
